I am Indian origin based in Toronto, Canada. I have been creative all of my life but started selling my paintings professionally only a few years ago. I started out posting on Instagram selling to friends and it has grown from there. Since childhood I have been surrounded by creativity as my mother is a wonderful artist herself. She introduced me to the tools and techniques at a very young age. My work is a mix of both Impressionism & Abstract styles of Oil & Acrylic paintings on Canvas & Wood. I intuitively choose my subjects and I use bright colors that represent a positive attitude and an awakened consciousness. Few artists that inspire me are Vincent VanGoh, Cezanne and Claud Monet. Their bold strokes and the use of unconventional colors that was once considered avant-garde helps me push the envelop a little more with every painting. Through my paintings I believe I am shortening the distance between my inner and outer worlds. Connecting with like minded people and sharing different interpretations of art motivates me to be creative everyday.

Thankyou for being a part of my dream!

Truly Grateful,
Kanchan Bhoj
